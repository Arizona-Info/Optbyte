BI and ETL Consulting

Through our Business Intelligence (BI) and ETL (Extract, Transform & Load) consulting services, we help our clients streamline and extract valuable insights from uncategorised data. Our technology-driven process helps our clients analyze data to make informed business decisions. As part of the BI process, organizations collect data from internal IT systems and external sources, prepare it for analysis, run queries against the data and create data visualizations. Further, through the creation of BI dashboards and reports, we make analytical results available to business users for operational decision-making and strategic planning.

These insights enable organizations to increase revenue, improve operational efficiency and a gain competitive advantage.

Our BI-ETL consulting solutions use a combination of analytics, data management and reporting tools, plus various methodologies for managing and analyzing data.

Our process

  • Data preparation: In this stage, we organise and model data sets analysis
  • Analytical querying: The prepared data is put through a set of analytical questions for review
  • Communication: The Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and other findings are communicated to key business users
  • Implementation: The extracted insights and findings are implemented to influence and drive key business decisions.
