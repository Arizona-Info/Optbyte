About OptByte

OptByte is one India’s leading providers of innovative software solutions that streamline complex business processes. We are committed to delivering best-in-class solutions that help our clients enhance the end-consumer experience. These solutions have been instrumental in ensuring customer retention and streamlining operations for enhanced productivity & efficiency.

We help world’s leading brands solve problems and set up processes today that help them transform tomorrow. We empower our clients to make better decisions with real-time AI and intelligent automations services.

OptByte has built scalable architecture and a low-code platform to stay ahead of the changing business scenario. Our customised solutions help our clients save time, thereby ensuring that the customer queries are resolved with minimum downtime.

We offer end-to-end software development services, preventive maintenance and management services. Our dedicated software development teams assist Startups, SMEs, and Enterprises across the globe with solutions related to Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, and Product development.

Our team comprises of highly qualified experts with in-depth knowledge of industry & business scenarios. Collectively, our team has a rich experience in industry to ensure swift & cost-effective query resolution.
